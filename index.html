My Journey

Ever since I was a child, I've had an inherent fascination with building things. Whether stacking blocks or tinkering with random things like disassembling and reassembling my pen, I felt like an engineer crafting a complex machine. For me, there was always a sense of joy in creating things. As I grew older, this interest naturally evolved, and when I stumbled upon coding, it felt like finding the perfect outlet for my creativity. What appealed to me most was that all I needed was a computer and a willingness to learn.

Over the years, I've explored my interests and passions through various avenues. I've volunteered in different organizations, from marketing to accounting, but it was programming that truly captured my heart. For me, it wasn't just about writing lines of code; it was about crafting solutions, bringing ideas to life, and making a tangible impact.

My journey in the world of technology has been marked by continuous exploration and learning. I've immersed myself in initiatives like Girls Who Code, Kode with Klossy, ASHS ProgVar, and UP Algo++, where I found a supportive community and endless opportunities to grow in this field. Participating in hackathons added an exhilarating dimension to my learning journey, pushing me to think creatively and collaborate effectively under pressure.

Aside from this, my love for programming grew even larger after reading a book by Al Sweigart, in which he always emphasized the power of coding.
